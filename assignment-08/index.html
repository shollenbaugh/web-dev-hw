This instructable will be teaching how to render a video using Sony Vegas Pro.

  1. After opening the program and your save file with the video inside, you will need to go into the File section, finding the Render As option.

  2. A box will appear with options for rendering settings you are able to adjust, like where the project will render to and what quality you want your video to render in.
  3. Once you have selected your settings you would like, clicking the render button will prompt your video to begin rendering, even adding a progress bar complete with a percentage counter and an estimated rendering time.
